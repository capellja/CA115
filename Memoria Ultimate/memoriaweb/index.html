Product Description

Our product is a headset made specifically for the eldery to help with memory loss or trauma using Virtual Reality (VR) technology. The headset is designed with multiple straps to accommodate for different head shapes.

Features

The VR headset comes pre-downloaded with multiple simulations and games that can be run in an offline setting. It also houses a WI-FI setting which will provide a direct connection to the doctor of the patient. This will allow them to communicate and allow the doctor to upload new material if needed.

What's in the box?

There will be a charger, controllers, headset and also complimentary stickers of our logo included within the packaging. Though extra accessorys can also be bought additionally. Enhanced resolution and 3D graphics are featured. 128 GB for storage allows for a series of simulations to be downloaded.

Our Mobile App

Our mobile app will soon be available to use. This app will let you conveniently keep track of your activities, details and stats using the headset. Activities like How much time was spent using the headset, a progress section and your most and least used features will also be kept track of on the app.
